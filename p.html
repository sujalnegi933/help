HOW TO LOG IN?

    Step 1

  • First of all, enter your name.

    • Step 2

  • After entering your username, the green 'Next' button will appear. Click on it, and you will proceed to the next step.

    • Step 3

  • Click on the 'Next' button. Once clicked, enter your email ID in the provided field. After entering your email, the 'Next' button will appear again. Click on it to proceed.

    • Step 4

  • After entering your email, the green 'Next' button should now be visible. Click on it to proceed to the next step.

    • Step 5

  • After entering your email, the green 'Next' button should appear. Click on it to move to the next step, where you can create a new and strong password of your choice. Once you've set your password, confirm it by entering it again. After this, the 'Next' button will appear once more. Click on it to proceed to the next step.

  • NOTE: Remember, the password you create should be at least 8 characters long.
  • After entering the password, click on the 'Next' button. Then, check the checkbox to agree to the terms and conditions. If you have filled in all the details correctly, your account will be successfully created.
